Team HeartBeats

My Heart Beats Does Yours?

You are alive only when your heart beats so loud that it rings like a voice in the ears needing some compassion so that all heart beats in the same harmony. With that kept in mind we have started this humble initiative of ours namely ''Heartbeats''.
India's main life-force and grassroots leads us back to our villages. We at heartbeats aim to mobilize this life-force with possible assistance garnered from every sources on to the path of sustainable development.
We specifically concentrate our forces of operation in the rural provinces catering to their specific needs in hope of turning into reality some harnessed dreams with the motto -'' My heart beats for you''.
Our main target areas are-
1) Working on primary education of children with real focus on expanding their realms of thinking with eyes on sensitizing them about real life issues including gender equality, environment conservation and other such issues needing critical thought.
2) Regular working with the local population with focus on spreading awareness regarding rural welfare directed government policies through assist of digitalization.
3) Identifying best farming techniques suited to the specific requirements of the cultivated vegetation with required assistance from all possible quarters.

There are many committed organizations that are doing commendable work for children, aged, environment, women, rural areas and in many other fields. Also, there are many corporate or organizations who would like to help non-profit organizations at a voluntary level and support their causes.
We act as bridge between the one who need help and the one who could help.
